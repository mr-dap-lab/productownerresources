# 📋 Product Owner Resources

> A growing collection of workshop materials, glossaries, and practical guides for anyone on the journey to becoming a stronger Product Owner.

Whether you're stepping into the role for the first time or sharpening your craft, this repo gathers the slides, handouts, and reference material I use to teach and practice modern product ownership.

## 🎯 Who this is for

- 🌱 **Aspiring Product Owners** preparing for the role or a certification (CSPO, PSPO, SAFe POPM)
- 🔄 **Practicing POs & Scrum Masters** who want refreshers and ready-to-use teaching material
- 👩‍🏫 **Facilitators & coaches** running their own product ownership workshops

---

## 🚀 How to use these

1. Start with the **deck** to get the big-picture view of the modern PO role.
2. Keep the **glossary** handy as a quick reference for terminology.
3. Work through the **handout** to apply the concepts to your own context.

Use them for self-study, team onboarding, or as a base for your own sessions — just keep attribution per the license.
